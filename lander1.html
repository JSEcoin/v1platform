Technical Brief

The code is copy and pasted on to your site and runs in the background after 10 seconds so as not to affect page load speeds. The visitor is notified with a privacy pop up the first time they visit the site in a similar way to the EU cookie notifications, this has links to a privacy page with an opt-out option. The code then uses excess CPU power to carry out mathematical hashing operations (SHA256) on data for our blockchain. Successfully found hashes are sent back to our servers with an Ajax call and the webmasters are rewarded with cryptocurrency coins.
